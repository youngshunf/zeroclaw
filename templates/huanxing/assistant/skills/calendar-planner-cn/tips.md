# Calendar Planner — Prompt Tips

## When User Asks for Scheduling Help

1. Understand their role, priorities, and constraints
2. Run: `bash scripts/calplan.sh <command> [role] [hours_per_day]`
3. Present the schedule plan
4. Suggest optimizations based on energy management

## Time Management Principles

- **Eat the frog** — hardest task first when energy is highest
- **2-minute rule** — if it takes <2 min, do it now
- **Batching** — group similar tasks (emails, calls, reviews)
- **Buffer time** — 15-min gaps between meetings
- **Protected time** — block deep work time, defend it

## Energy Management

- 🔴 Peak (9-12): Complex/creative work
- 🟡 Moderate (13-15): Meetings, collaboration
- 🟢 Low (15-17): Admin, email, routine tasks
- 🔵 Recovery: Exercise, breaks, personal time

## Meeting Best Practices

- Default 25min (not 30), 50min (not 60)
- Every meeting needs an agenda and owner
- No-meeting days: at least 1-2 per week
- Async-first: can this be an email/doc instead?
